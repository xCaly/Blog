I volunteered on our local COVID-19 research and testing center! The government looked for several volunteers up for this task and I signed myself up! We were intensively trained for more than 2 weeks about safety, hygiene and protocol. I met a lot of brave and friendly researchers, microbiologists, nurses, doctors and other volunteers! I I helped a lot of people and it makes me feel good. There are even times that companies who go to our testing center will give us food and freebies! We aren’t getting paid for doing this, and I dont mind that all because this is a great learning experience for me. My favorite part of my volunteering adventure is during Eid where everyone prepared for an indoor festival on the building and different teams contribute their own presentation. There were giveaways from several companies and girls were even given roses as a token of appreciation of what we do. We have different zones here to protect the people from those who have covid and each zone has their designated color. I am from the yellow zone, where people come back to get tested again hoping that they’d get a 2nd negative result so they can take out their watch tracker(a device used by the government to track people with COVID-19). I enjoyed this experience very much and I’m thankful I helped a lot of people.
